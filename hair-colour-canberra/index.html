Colour backed by science

Koleston Perfect ME+ features Wella's exclusive ME+ molecule — the result of seven years of research — which reduces the risk of developing new colour allergies by up to 60 times. Do you do grey coverage?

Yes — Koleston Perfect ME+ delivers up to 100% grey coverage, and we also offer grey blending if you'd rather soften than cover.
