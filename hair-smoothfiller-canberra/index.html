What is SmoothFiller?

SmoothFiller is a demi-permanent smoothing treatment from Wella Professionals. Its Smart Delivery System carries active ingredients deep into the hair cortex, rebuilding internal bonds for hair that's smoother, stronger and dramatically easier to manage. Unlike traditional keratin treatments there's no harsh regrowth line, no special shampoo rules, and no waiting days before you can wash your hair.

Who is it for?

Anyone dealing with frizz, flyaways or hard-to-manage hair — especially if you reach for the straightener or blow-dryer daily. SmoothFiller preserves your natural texture: waves and curls keep their shape, just smoother and more defined. Results are cumulative, so the more treatments you have, the smoother your hair becomes. It's safe for colour-treated hair and one of the few treatments of its kind offered in Canberra.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SmoothFiller last?

Up to 3 months, depending on your hair type and washing frequency. Results build with repeat treatments.

Will it straighten my curls?

No — SmoothFiller smooths and de-frizzes while keeping your natural curl pattern. If you want permanently straight hair, ask us about nanoplasty or Shiseido straightening instead.

Is it safe for coloured hair?

Yes. SmoothFiller is formaldehyde-free and safe for colour-treated hair — many clients combine it with a colour service in the same visit.

How long does the appointment take?

Around 90 minutes to 2 hours depending on hair length and density.
