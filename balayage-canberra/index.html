Why choose balayage?

Balayage is a freehand colour technique where lightener is painted onto the surface of the hair for a soft, natural gradient — brighter through the ends, seamlessly blended at the root. Because there's no hard regrowth line, it's one of the lowest-maintenance colour services you can choose: most clients refresh every 3–4 months instead of every 6 weeks. It suits almost every base colour and length, and our stylists tailor placement to your face shape and how you wear your hair.

The Hair Avenue difference

Every balayage at Hair Avenue includes a personalised consultation, Wella Blondor lightening with Wellaplex bond protection, a custom toner to nail your exact tone — warm honey, beachy bronde or cool ash — and a polished blow wave finish. Our Braddon salon uses Wella Professionals exclusively, and our colourists are blonde specialists first and foremost.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does balayage take?

Allow 2.5–4 hours depending on hair length, thickness and how much lift we're creating. Ultra blonde and corrective work can take longer — we'll give you an accurate time at your free consultation.

How often do I need to redo balayage?

Most clients book a refresh every 12–16 weeks. Because the colour is hand-painted with a soft root blend, it grows out without a harsh line.

Will balayage damage my hair?

We use Wella Blondor with Wellaplex bond protection, which reduces breakage by up to 97% compared to traditional lightening. Your stylist will assess your hair's condition first and recommend the safest path to your goal.

Do you offer a free balayage consultation?

Yes — colour consultations are complimentary. We'll assess your hair, discuss tones and placement, and give you an exact quote before you commit.
