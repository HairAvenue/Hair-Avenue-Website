Wella SmoothFiller — Smooth. Fill. Restructure. from $249

Tired of fighting frizz every morning? SmoothFiller by Wella Professionals is a revolutionary demi-permanent treatment that transforms your hair in a single salon session. Its Smart Delivery System carries active ingredients deep into the cortex, building new bonds from the inside out for smoothness that lasts up to three months.

Unlike traditional keratin treatments, SmoothFiller preserves your natural texture — your waves and curls keep their shape, just smoother and infinitely more manageable. There's no harsh regrowth line, no sulfate-free shampoo rules, and no waiting days to wash. You leave the chair with humidity-proof hair, faster blow-dry time, and a style that holds.

Who it's for: Anyone dealing with frizz and hard-to-manage hair — especially if you find yourself reaching for the straightener or blow-dryer daily. Results are cumulative, so the more you do it, the smoother it gets.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Is Hair Avenue walk-in friendly? +
Yes! While we recommend booking online to guarantee your preferred time and stylist, walk-ins are always welcome when availability allows. We'll do our best to fit you in — we've even been known to squeeze in last-minute visitors from out of town!
Where can I park near Hair Avenue in Braddon? +
Free street parking is available along Lonsdale Street and surrounding streets in the Braddon precinct. There's also paid parking at the nearby Braddon car park. The salon is at Unit 102/16 Lonsdale Street — look for us at street level.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), EFTPOS, and contactless payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay. We want to make payment as easy as possible!
Do you offer a free consultation before colour services? +
Absolutely! We offer complimentary consultations for colour services and major changes. It's the perfect opportunity to discuss your vision, assess your hair's condition, and get an accurate quote. You can book a free consultation directly through our online booking system.
Is Hair Avenue LGBTQ+ friendly? +
100%. Hair Avenue is a proudly inclusive, LGBTQ+ friendly salon. Everyone is welcome and celebrated here, exactly as they are. Our team is committed to creating a safe, comfortable, and affirming space for all clients.
How do I book an appointment? +
You can book online 24/7 through our Phorest booking system — just click any "Book Now" button on our website. You can also call us directly at 0449 801 937, or simply walk in! Online booking lets you choose your preferred stylist, service, date, and time.
What is Hair SmoothFiller and how long does it last? +
Hair SmoothFiller is a deep conditioning treatment that infuses your hair with proteins and vitamins, repairing damage, eliminating frizz, and restoring incredible shine. It's formaldehyde-free and safe for colour-treated hair. Results typically last 8-12 weeks. It's one of our most popular services and something most Canberra salons don't offer.
What is nanoplasty? +
Nanoplasty is an advanced smoothing treatment that uses nanotechnology to penetrate deep into the hair fibre, eliminating frizz and adding lasting shine without harsh chemicals. It's ideal for curly, wavy, or unruly hair types. The treatment is gentle enough for most hair and delivers silky, manageable results that last for months.
Do you do kids' haircuts? +
Yes! We welcome children and families. Our team creates a relaxed, fun environment so kids feel comfortable. We understand little ones can be wriggly — no judgement here! We're also a great choice for a family booking so everyone can get their hair done together.
