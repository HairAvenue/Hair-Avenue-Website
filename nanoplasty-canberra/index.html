What is nanoplasty?

Nanoplasty uses nano-sized molecules of amino acids and proteins that penetrate deep into the hair fibre, restructuring it from within. The result is dramatically smoother, straighter, glossier hair — without the formaldehyde used in traditional keratin straightening. It's ideal for curly, wavy, frizzy or unruly hair types, and gentle enough for most hair conditions.

Nanoplasty, SmoothFiller or permanent straightening?

If you want months of sleek, low-maintenance hair, nanoplasty is your treatment. If you love your waves but hate frizz, Wella SmoothFiller smooths while preserving texture. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does nanoplasty last?

Typically 4–6 months depending on hair type and aftercare. Regrowth comes in naturally with no harsh demarcation line.

Is nanoplasty better than keratin?

Nanoplasty is formaldehyde-free and works by restructuring the hair fibre with amino acids rather than coating it, so results look more natural and the treatment is gentler on your hair and scalp.

Can I colour my hair as well?

Yes — our stylists regularly pair nanoplasty with colour. We'll plan the order and timing of services at your consultation to protect your hair.

How long does the appointment take?

Allow 2.5–3.5 hours depending on length and density.
