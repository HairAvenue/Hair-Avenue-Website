Why regular regrowth keeps colour looking its best

As your hair grows, the line between fresh growth and your coloured lengths becomes more visible — usually around the 4–6 week mark for all-over colour. A regrowth service refreshes just the roots, blending them seamlessly into your existing shade so the whole head reads even and intentional again. Staying on a regular regrowth schedule is gentler on your hair than letting roots grow out and doing a full colour each time, and it keeps your look polished for special occasions, work, or simply feeling like yourself.

The Hair Avenue difference

Every regrowth service uses Wella Koleston Perfect ME+ — the world’s most advanced professional permanent colour — for up to 100% grey coverage and rich, true-to-tone results with significantly less damage. We finish with a toner where needed and a polished blow wave, so you leave with colour that looks freshly done from root to tip. Not sure whether you need a simple regrowth or a fuller refresh? A free consultation will sort it in minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my regrowth done?

Most clients book every 4–6 weeks for all-over colour, and every 6–8 weeks for blonde scalp bleach regrowth. We’ll recommend a schedule based on your hair’s growth and how visible your roots are.

What’s the difference between regrowth and a full colour?

Regrowth refreshes only the new growth at your roots and blends it into your existing colour — quicker and gentler than re-colouring the whole head. If your lengths have faded too, the Regrowth + All Over Toner package refreshes both.

Do you cover greys?

Yes — Wella Koleston Perfect ME+ delivers up to 100% grey coverage. If you’d prefer to soften rather than fully cover, ask us about grey blending instead.

Do I need a consultation first?

For regular regrowth maintenance you can book straight in. For a first visit or if you’re changing shade, a free consultation lets us match your colour precisely.
